    Rules of the game:
  • Main goal of Blackjack is to get 21 or more than the croupier.
  • Click “Start New Game” to start a completely new game with a new card deck.
  • Click “New Round” if you want to start the new round with the same card deck (cards from the previous turn do not take part in the new turn).
  • At the beginning of the game both player and croupier get a card (you can’t see the croupier card)
  • The first turn is yours, you can click
    • Hit – if you want to take another card from the deck;
    • Pass – if you think you have enough points give a turn to the croupier.
  • After your turn, the croupier gets new cards to the moment when he will have 21 or more points than you (but less 21 of course).
  • If you have 21 points, you automatically win this round.
  • If you have above 21 points you lose and the croupier gets a point.
  • Cards points – each card has a different value:
    • Cards of rank 2 – 10 have the same points mean 2-10
    • All face cards (Jack, Queen, King) have 10 points
    • Aces have 11 points
